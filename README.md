📊 Financial Dashboard | Revenue Analysis (2019–2020) | Power BI
Excited to share my latest Financial Dashboard built in Power BI, where I analyzed revenue performance for the 2019–2020 financial year.
In this project, I started by transforming and cleaning the dataset, then created the required measures and KPIs, including:
💰 Total Revenue
📈 Average Revenue
🌍 Total Countries
🧾 Total Transactions
After preparing the data, I selected the most effective visualizations to present key business insights:
🔹 Card Visuals with custom logo for KPI highlights
🔹 Donut Chart to analyze total revenue by region
🔹 Line & Clustered Column Chart to compare Total Revenue and Average Revenue across countries and regions
🔹 Treemap to visualize revenue contribution by different stores
🔹 Ribbon Chart to compare rankings over time
🔹 Area Chart to identify revenue trends
🔹 Matrix (Table) Visual for detailed data analysis
🔹 Heat Map to quickly identify high- and low-performing areas
🔹 Interactive Slicers with customized formatting for dynamic filtering
This project helped me strengthen my skills in:
✅ Data Transformation (Power Query)
✅ Data Modeling
✅ DAX Calculations
✅ KPI Design
✅ Interactive Dashboard Development
✅ Data Visualization & Business Insights
